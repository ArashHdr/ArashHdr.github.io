Course Extension form

Before Your Start

If you feel you need more time to complete your course you may apply for an extension. Based on your eligibility, the amount of time that is extended for will be added on from your course expiry date.

  • Make sure you have familiarised yourself with your terms of enrolment
  • You will be informed of your eligibility as part of this application
  • Have your payment details on hand, you will be required to pay upfront for the amount of time you extend by.
