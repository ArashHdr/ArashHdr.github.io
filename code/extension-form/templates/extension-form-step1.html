Course Extension T&Cs and Declaration

Please ensure you read and understand the following conditions.

You must submit your application for extension prior to the course expiry date and ensure all course fees due have been paid prior to the extension request.
If your request is successful, you will be required to pay a course extension fee outlined in the Schedule of Administrative Fees. A maximum of six months may be granted and Open Colleges reserves the right to refuse an application for extension. You will be required to pay a course extension fee of $50 AUD per month, as outlined in the Schedule of Administrative Fees.

Open Colleges reserves the right to refuse an application for extension where we do not deem this to be in your best interest.
